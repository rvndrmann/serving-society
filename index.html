How We Support You

At Serving Society, we believe every person deserves the freedom to live life fully. Our approach is rooted in compassion, dignity, and genuine partnership.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, compassionate support services that empower NDIS participants to achieve independence, dignity, and meaningful community participation.

Our Vision

A society where every person with disability has equal access to opportunities, resources, and support to live a self-determined, fulfilling life.

Our Values

Respect, Integrity, Inclusivity, Excellence, and Collaboration. Everything we do is guided by person-centred practice and the NDIS Practice Standards.

01

Person-Centred Planning

Your goals, your choices. We work alongside you to design support that reflects your unique needs and aspirations.

02

Qualified & Caring Staff

Our team is thoroughly screened, trained in the NDIS framework, and genuinely passionate about making a difference.

03

Transparent Communication

Clear, honest conversations about your plan, funding, and progress — always keeping you and your family informed.

04

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and refine our services to ensure the highest standards of care, safety, and outcomes.

Our NDIS Services

Comprehensive, flexible support across every stage of your NDIS journey — from daily living assistance to complex specialist care.

01

In-Home Support

Skilled, compassionate support within your home — helping you live comfortably and maintain your independence every day.

  • Supported Independent Living (SIL)
  • Daily Personal Activities
  • High Intensity Daily Activities
  • Household Tasks
  • Short Term Accommodation (STA)
02

Community Access

Connecting you with your community — supporting meaningful social participation and increased independence outside the home.

  • Community Participation
  • Group & Centre-Based Activities
  • Assistance with Travel & Transport
03

Capacity Building

Building the skills, confidence, and systems you need to achieve greater independence over the long term.

  • Specialised Support Coordination
  • Tenancy Assistance
  • Life Skills Development
04

Therapeutic Supports

Evidence-based therapeutic interventions delivered by qualified practitioners to support your health, wellbeing, and functional goals.

  • Allied Health Services
  • Behaviour Support
  • Psychology & Counselling
  • Occupational Therapy
05

Restrictive Practices

Lawful, ethical, and rights-based management of regulated restrictive practices — always with authorisation and with the goal of elimination.

  • Behaviour Support Plans
  • Regulated Practice Authorisation
  • Rights & Dignity Safeguards
  • Reduction & Elimination Planning
06

SDA

Specialist Disability Accommodation designed and built to meet the needs of participants with extreme functional impairment or very high support needs.

  • Improved Liveability
  • Fully Accessible Housing
  • Robust Design
  • High Physical Support
